A Little about US

Founded in 1995, Sam's Culinary Canopy has been serving authentic Italian cuisine for over 25 years. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring an authentic taste that suit you in every bite.

We source our ingredients from local farmers and import specialty items directly from Nigeria and also abroad to maintain the highest quality standards.
